Homemade Burger Recipe

The best bison, beef, and pork burger that you will ever taste

I first discovered this recipe while stationed in Germany some years ago. My roomate at the time was a very good cook and I only knew how to cook apaghetti. I had told him that I loved burgers but only ever had beef burgers. Shocked, he offered to make a bison burger for me. I was skeptical at first but as soon as the burger hit my taste buds I knew there was no going back to a burger made any other way.

INGREDIENTS

measurements for seasoning is accoreding to how much seasoned you want the meat

  • 1/3 lb ground beef
  • 1/3 lb ground bison
  • 1/3 lb ground pork
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • smoked paprika(normal paprika works too)
  • a pinch of lemon pepper
  • cooking oil
  • chedar cheese

STEPS

  1. Mix bison, beef, and pork together in large bowl
  2. Season mixed meat with seasoning as desired
  3. Shape seasoned meat into patties
  4. Cover and place patties into firdge overnight
  5. Pore small amount of oil into pan over medium heat until heated
  6. Place patties in pan individual or more than one at a time depending on the size of pan
  7. Cook on one side for about 10 minutes then flip sides
  8. Place cheese slices on top of patties and cook until melted
  9. Remove patties from heat and move to burger preperation

BURGER PREPERATION

The preperation of the bun and the topings of the burger are just as important as cooking the burger itself.

  1. Toast bun on pan used for burger patties
  2. Spread perfered sauce on both sides of the bun
  3. Place lettuce on the bottom of the bun
  4. Place patty on top of lettuce
  5. Place any other topping on top of patty followed by top bun
