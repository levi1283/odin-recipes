Crockpot Chili Recipe

An easy and great tasting crockpot chili

This recipe is a great one if you don't feel like doing too much but also want a great warm meal that is sure to produce leftovers.

INGREDIENTS

Measurements are according to how seasoned you want your chili.

  • 1lb Ground beef
  • 1 can toatoes
  • 1 large can of tomatoe sauce
  • 2 cans of chili beans
  • salt
  • paprika
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cayenne pepper

Optional ingredients

  • Rice
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Tortilla chips (crushed)

STEPS

  1. Brown ground beef in pan with oil
  2. Season beef while browning
  3. Add ground beef and all canned ingredients together in crockpot
  4. Season ingredients in crockpot as desired
  5. Set crockpot to high heat for 4 hours
  6. Cover and leave alone
  7. Uncover and stir ingredients
  8. Add chease to individual bowls or in crockpot
  9. Add rice to indivual bowl then add chili on top
  10. garnish with crushed tortilla chips
