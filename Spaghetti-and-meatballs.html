SPAGHETTI AND MEATBALLS RECIPES

Spaghetti and meatballs is a classic recipe that has been around a long time. This recipe is my persnal favorite take on the recipe.

INGREDIENTS

The ingredients are for the meatballs only.

  • 1lb ground italian sausage
  • Italian seasoning
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Ground garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Parmesan

STEPS

  1. Mix everything besides the seasonings into large mixing bowl
  2. After everything is mixed add in seasonings
  3. Shape into meatballs
  4. Add oil to pan and over medium heat
  5. Cook meatballs in pan rotating in order to cook entirely
  6. Place meatballs to sauce and noodles, and enjoy
